Our Core Principles

Disciplined Approach

We invest with a focus on intrinsic value and a clear margin of safety. Every decision is guided by business fundamentals, sound reasoning, and an insistence on rational capital allocation.

Long-Term Horizon

We think like business owners, not traders. Our goal is to hold outstanding companies that can compound value for decades, allowing time and quality to do the heavy lifting.

Partnership Mindset

We treat our partners’ capital as our own. The General Partner invests alongside the Limited Partners, ensuring alignment of interests and a shared commitment to long-term results.

Local and Global

While based in Indonesia, our search for value knows no borders. We invest where enduring businesses and sensible prices meet—whether at home or abroad.

Our Investment Focus

Our investment philosophy is built on a foundation of risk control, consistency, and a commitment to fundamental analysis. We believe that superior returns are achieved by taking a patient, long-term view.

We seek to make long-term, concentrated investments in high-quality, publicly-listed businesses. Our focus is on identifying wonderful companies with durable competitive advantages, led by honest and able management, at a time when their market price is significantly below their intrinsic value.

About Ganetika Capital Partners

Ganetika Capital is a private investment partnership focused on the ownership of high-quality businesses. The partnership invests in both Indonesian and global markets, seeking opportunities where market price diverges meaningfully from intrinsic value. Ganetika Capital applies a disciplined approach built on sound business judgment, rational capital allocation, and a partnership mindset. The partnership currently consists of one General Partner and four Limited Partners.
