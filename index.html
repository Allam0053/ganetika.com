Our Core Principles

Disciplined Approach

We invest with a focus on intrinsic value and a clear margin of safety. Every decision is guided by business fundamentals, sound reasoning, and an insistence on rational capital allocation.

Long-Term Horizon

We think like business owners, not traders. Our goal is to hold outstanding companies that can compound value for decades, allowing time and quality to do the heavy lifting.

Partnership Mindset

We treat our partners’ capital as our own. The General Partner invests alongside the Limited Partners, ensuring alignment of interests and a shared commitment to long-term results.

Local and Global

While based in Indonesia, our search for value knows no borders. We invest where enduring businesses and sensible prices meet—whether at home or abroad.

Our Investment Focus

Our approach rests on a few enduring ideas: protect the downside, stay consistent, and let the numbers and the business do the talking. We believe lasting results come from patience, discipline, and owning businesses we’d be happy to hold indefinitely.

We look to invest in a handful of exceptional (or at least great), publicly-listed companies. The kind run by honest, capable people, with strong competitive moats, and the ability to compound value over time. When the market offers these businesses at a price far below what they’re truly worth, we buy.

On occasion, when the opportunity is too compelling to ignore, we may also buy fair businesses at wonderful prices. These are not the core of the portfolio, but they can offer outsized returns when bought with a sufficient margin of safety.

About Ganetika Capital Partners

Ganetika Capital is a private investment partnership focused on the long-term ownership of high-quality businesses. The partnership invests in both Indonesian and global markets, seeking opportunities where price and value diverge. Our approach is rooted in sound business judgment, disciplined capital allocation, and a genuine partnership ethos. Ganetika Capital currently consists of one General Partner and four Limited Partners.
